GRUMPY SHARK HAT CAT

MAX DMG 70 HP 1200

The GRUMPY SHARK HAT CAT’s basic attack “paw slap” will deal 15 damage points to its opponent. The GSHC can perform a paw slap every 2 seconds. For its ultimate attack “stare of death” the GSHC uses its hat to transform into a hybrid shark-cat, gaining +60% grumpiness and deals 70 damage points to its opponent. The cooldown of 18 seconds can be reduced to 15 seconds if the ultimate attack is performed on a dog-type enemy.
